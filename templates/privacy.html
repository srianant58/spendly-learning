Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense records you create, including amount, category, date, and any description you add.
  • Basic usage data such as login timestamps, used only to maintain session security.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To maintain the integrity and performance of the application.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers and is accessible only through your authenticated account. Passwords are stored using industry-standard one-way hashing — we never store or transmit your password in plain text. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use essential infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are contractually bound to handle your data only as needed to deliver the service.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channels. We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries as promptly as possible.
